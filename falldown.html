Falldown

I wrote fall down for fun and to learn JavaScript and the Canvas HTML5 element.

Use right and left arrow or `a' and `d' to move the ball right and left. `r' and Backspace to restart. Space and `p' to pause. You may need to tab at the arrows to move the ball. May not work in chrome, should work in all firefoxes.


Please use a browser that supports HTML5s canvas tag
